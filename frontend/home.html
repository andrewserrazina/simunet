How SimuNet works

SimuNet captures every event from your sim hardware and shares it with your crew in real time. Whether you are running a virtual airline, VTC, or convoy meetup, the network keeps everyone in sync with no server wrangling required.

1

Connect your favorite sim

Install a lightweight connector for MSFS, X-Plane, DCS, Euro Truck, or American Truck Simulator and share live telemetry with one click.

2

Layer in dynamic events

Drop in crosswinds, traffic, radio chatter, and deliveries from the SimuNet library or remix your own mission packs to keep every run fresh.

3

Fly and drive together

Track shared dashboards, get alert pings, and replay the run from any angle so your squad can debrief and improve every mission.

FAQ

Questions simmers ask before joining

If you need something more specific, reach us at alpha@simunet.io.

Who is the Alpha for?

Dedicated flight sim pilots, trucking convoys, dispatchers, streamers, and community organizers who want a shared mission dashboard without managing servers.

What does onboarding include?

We hop on a call, help you install connectors, tune data for your sim hardware, and import your favorite flight plans or convoy routes.

Is there a cost to participate?

Alpha members pay a reduced monthly fee that locks in Founder pricing for launch. You can cancel anytime during the trial.

Does it work with my setup?

Yes. SimuNet runs alongside your existing add-ons on Windows PCs. Multi-PC cockpits, motion rigs, VR, and popular voice apps are fully supported.
