Why teams choose SimuNet

Built for operations teams that need to move faster than traditional simulation stacks allow. SimuNet gives you the situational awareness, automation, and safety guardrails required to ship reliable autonomous systems.

Unified mission view

Combine map, telemetry, and mission logs in a single view. Customizable overlays let pilots, dispatchers, and engineers collaborate without losing context.

Scenario automation

Trigger synthetic weather, payload changes, or emergency drills via a library of reusable scenarios. Validate readiness continuously, not just during quarterly drills.

Observability out of the box

Structured data export, long-term storage, and integrations with tools like Snowflake, Databricks, and Grafana keep your telemetry actionable.

Secure, enterprise-ready

Single-tenant deployment, SSO/SAML, audit logging, and fine-grained access control designed for regulated industries.

Edge to cloud

Deploy gateways close to your fleet, buffer data in the field, and replay missions in the cloud with deterministic timing.

Collaborative workflows

Assign missions, track approvals, and share instant replays with ops partners, regulators, or customers in a single interface.
